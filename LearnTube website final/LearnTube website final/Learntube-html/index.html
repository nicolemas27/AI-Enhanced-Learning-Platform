Our Features

Make the most out your Youtube learning experience

Learntube elevates your YouTube learning by offering summaries, quizzes, multilingual translations, real-time notes generation, and instant doubt solving. It seamlessly converts lengthy videos into digestible content in your preferred language, generates detailed notes for easy reference, and provides on-the-spot answers to your questions while you watch. With Learntube, every video becomes an interactive and personalized learning journey, perfectly tailored to your pace and style.

  • Quiz Generation

    Our quiz generator lets you adjust difficulty levels for a personalized challenge. From beginner to expert, tailor quizzes to match your learning needs!

  • Summary

    Our AI-powered summarizer generates concise summaries with key points, ensuring you capture the most important information. Customize the level of detail to fit your needs, from brief overviews to in-depth insights!

  • Flashcards

    Our smart flashcard generator creates customized flashcards with key concepts, helping you learn efficiently. Adjust difficulty levels to match your learning pace and retention needs!

  • Acceleral

    Our AI-powered chatbot instantly answers your doubts with clear and accurate explanations. Get personalized assistance anytime to enhance your learning experience!

  • Concept Maps

    Our concept map generator creates visual diagrams that connect key ideas, making complex topics easy to understand. Customize and explore relationships to enhance your learning!

  • multilingual Support

    Our platform offers multilingual support, allowing you to learn and interact in your preferred language. Break language barriers and access content effortlessly!
